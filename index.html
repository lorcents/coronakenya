About this site

This site was made with 2 purposes in mind.

  • First, to be able to provide an up to date overview of the current status of the country Kenya at a glance with the appropriate visualizations.

  • And two, to enable our fellow countrymen be able to track their own status on the platform. This way one might be able to see the progression of their symptoms and with the correct data, know at what stage they are in for statistical purposes.
